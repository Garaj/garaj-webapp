Garaj is a coworking space for hackers, makers and tinkerers in Hisarustu, Istanbul.
We love to create and hack software and hardware.

Most of us are students at Bogazici University, but we come from all walks of life and share our knowledge and create together in this special place. Free as in speech and free as in beer! You do not have to pay anything for building things at Garaj, and this is a special place where we love to hear interesting ideas from creative people like you!
